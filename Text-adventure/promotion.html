"I'll take the promotion," Steve says. "That battle was the most thrilling thing I've done in a long time."

"Okay, I'll make you my personal assistant. That means you'll get your own office, and you'll accompany me on all my missions."

"Sounds great! When do I start?"

"We'll get you started tomorrow. Now go get some rest."



Steve woke up the next day and prepared for his new job. When he got there, he found everyone waiting for him with a cake that said "Congratulations Steve" on a table.

The captain asked Steve, "What are you going to do after the assistant job?"

Steve said, "I'm not sure. I'll find something though."

Steve lived out the rest of his days as an assistant.
